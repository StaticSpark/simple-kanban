Simple Kanban - Demo

This is a very lightweight Project Dashboard (I guess it could also be used for something else. Goal: Easy and fast to edit. Idea originally came from Stephan Schmidt, but since he was missing a simple persistency feature I started building it from scratch and because I didn't like the one-file-solution I did a complete rewrite.

Probably best to checkout the Demo (disabled persistency feature).
